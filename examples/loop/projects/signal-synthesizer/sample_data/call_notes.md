# Fieldwork User Research — Q1 2026

## Call 1 — Rachel Torres, Operations Manager, CleanPro Services
Date: 2026-01-14 | 25 min

Rachel manages a team of 18 field cleaners. Signed up 6 weeks ago, still onboarding.

The main issue: she spent two hours trying to import her existing customer list on day one and gave up. "There's a step where you upload a CSV and it just said 'error' with no explanation. I tried three times. I still haven't got my customer data in." She's been manually entering jobs one by one since. Would have churned already but her manager pushed her to persist.

Also asked for better reporting: "I need to show my boss a weekly summary of completed jobs and hours. I just screenshot the screen right now."

---

## Call 2 — Dan Whitfield, sole trader, HVAC repairs
Date: 2026-01-21 | 18 min

Dan is a one-person operation. Uses Fieldwork on his phone mostly. "The app is decent but when I try to import my old jobs from my spreadsheet it crashes. I just gave up and started fresh."

Wants a job summary report he can send to clients. "Something I can email that looks professional, not a screenshot."

Doesn't have a team, so team features are irrelevant. "I don't need any of the team stuff."

---

## Call 3 — Priya Mehta, Service Director, QuickFix Plumbing
Date: 2026-02-03 | 30 min

Priya manages 6 plumbers. Happy overall with route optimisation — "that bit actually works well." Main frustration: data import. "We spent half a day with your CSV template and we couldn't get our customer addresses to import properly. We had to type everything in manually."

Also mentioned wanting better reports: "I want to pull a monthly revenue report without having to export to Excel and format it myself."

No mention of team collaboration features ("we just use WhatsApp for that") or API integrations.

---

## Summary of themes

**Mentioned by 3/3 users:**
- Data import (step 3) is broken or confusing — they give up and enter data manually

**Mentioned by 3/3 users:**
- Want better reporting / export — weekly summaries, client-facing PDFs, revenue reports

**Not mentioned by any user:**
- Team collaboration features
- Mobile app quality (they use it, just don't complain about it)
- API integrations
